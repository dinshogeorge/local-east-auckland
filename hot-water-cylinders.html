Symptoms of a Faulty Hot Water Cylinder

  • Not heating
  • High Power bill
  • Foul smell
  • Strange noises
  • Delay in producing hot water
  • Rusty hot water cylinder
  • Leaks in the cylinder
  • Low temperature

If you notice any of these signs, don't panic. Just seek professional assistance at the earliest for the repair, replacement, or new installation of your existing hot water cylinder.

Hot water cylinder replacement and installation

Most of the hot water cylinders last less than 10 years. It is usually recommended to replace the system after 10 years as you will start to see the signs for a replacement or an upgrade to the existing one. We only install high-quality hot water cylinders from top brands like Rheem, Bosch, Vulcan, and Rinnai which offer more longevity than others.
