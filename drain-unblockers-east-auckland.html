Drain repair and replacement services

No matter how your drain has become damaged or clogged, we are here to help with the needful repairs. The East Auckland Plumbers have highly qualified drainage experts that protect your home from the dangers of clogged drainage with a wide range of the latest plumbing equipment.

1. Drain repair and replacement services

For inspecting the damaged drain, we use the most modern technology, CCTV camera equipment to trace down the origin of the leak and then suggest solutions to fix the issue efficiently.

2. Drain jetting

To clear out all kinds of blockages in drains, drain jetting is used by which high-pressure water clears the dirt and other particles accumulated in the drains. With this non-invasive drain cleaning system, we help you unblock your drains and keep them in the best possible condition.

3. Tree root cutting

When it comes to severe blockages in drains, tree root invasion is a major concern. Tree roots entering the drainage widens the existing cracks in the pipelines leading to rapid growth in the drains. Here, at East Auckland Plumber we can help save your time and money with early detection and tree root cutting before it causes significant damage to your drainage system.

4. Drain relining

For drain relining, our experienced set of plumbers repair by inserting a new pipe into the damaged drainage pipes forming a protective layer to your drains. We do this in the safest and environment-friendly manner without excavating the whole area.

Common causes of Drain Blockage

Blockages start with minor issues and slowly turn into serious problems in the drainage system. So recognizing the causes of clogged drains can help in eliminating such blockages in the near future.

Some of the common causes of drain blockage are
  • Dirt
  • Hair
  • Soap
  • Food waste
  • Toilet paper build-up
  • Mineral build-up
  • Tree roots

With regular maintenance and services, we can help you unblock the drains to prevent serious damage caused.
